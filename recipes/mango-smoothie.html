Mango Smoothie

Description

Make this Yogurt-Free Mango Smoothie recipe for a filling breakfast that will help you begin your day by taking good care of your body from the inside out!

Ingredients

  • Fresh or Frozen Mango.
  • Frozen banana.

Steps:

  1. Blend chopped mango, banana slices, ice-cold water, in a high-speed blender and blend until smooth and you reach the desired consistency (about 40 seconds).
  2. Then pour the smoothie into a tall glass and optionally garnish with mint leaves.
